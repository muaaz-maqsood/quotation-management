Why Web App Owns Everything Until Push
  • Pre-sales iterates dozens of times per quote — cluttering Odoo with scratchpad data breaks audit hygiene.
  • AI / voice is the future primary interface — a purpose-built web app is the right home for this; Odoo UI cannot evolve fast enough.
  • Approvals can use custom SLAs, threads, diffs — Odoo's native approvals are generic and rigid.
  • Odoo stays clean — only approved quotes become sale orders, so finance & project teams see one source of truth.
  • Read-only from Odoo is cheap & safe — masters rarely change; bearer-token GETs with cache handle 99% of lookups.
